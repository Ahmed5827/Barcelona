About Us

Founded in 1899, FC Barcelona is more than a football club — it is a symbol of passion, identity, and sporting excellence. Known worldwide as Barça, the club has built a proud legacy of success on the pitch, with countless domestic and international titles, while staying true to its values of creativity, teamwork, and social responsibility. Rooted deeply in Catalan culture, FC Barcelona represents “Més que un club” (“More than a club”), uniting millions of fans across the globe with its iconic style of play, legendary players, and unwavering commitment to community and innovation in sport.

Our Stadium
The club’s home is the historic Spotify Camp Nou, Europe’s largest stadium, which has been an emblem of FC Barcelona since its inauguration in 1957. More than just a sporting venue, Camp Nou is a cultural landmark where unforgettable football moments are created and shared. It has hosted legendary matches, witnessed generations of star players, and continues to be a place of pilgrimage for supporters from all over the world — a true symbol of the unity and spirit that defines Barça.
