Our Stadium

The club’s home is the historic Spotify Camp Nou, Europe’s largest stadium, which has been an emblem of FC Barcelona since its inauguration in 1957.

More than just a sporting venue, Camp Nou is a cultural landmark where unforgettable football moments are created and shared. It has hosted legendary matches, witnessed generations of star players, and continues to be a place of pilgrimage for supporters from all over the world — a true symbol of the unity and spirit that defines Barça.
